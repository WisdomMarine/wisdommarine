POLICIES

Safety Policy

Wisdom Marine Services L.L.C is committed to providing framework for setting occupational health, safety and environmental performance objectives.

  • To achieve continues improvement in the HSE management system through education, training, consultation, reward and recognition programs, auditing, constant monitoring, evaluation and review.
  • Ensure the health and safety of workforce, the environment, related parties and public by adhering to the local/national/international legal and other requirements.

Customer Service Policy

  • Provide progress updates at agreed intervals.
  • Maintain open communication for technical queries and change requests.
  • Commit to warranty repairs for workmanship defects within agreed period.

Quality Policy

  • Provide our customers with the best value for our products and services.
  • Strive for continual improvement by setting quality objectives and targets
  • Lead the employees to work as a team and create an environment to obtain better business results.
  • Comply with applicable statutory and regulatory requirements related to products and services.
  • Continually comply with the requirements of ISO 9001-2008 and improve the effectiveness of our quality management system.
